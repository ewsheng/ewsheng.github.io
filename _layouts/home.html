About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California, advised by Dr. Prem Natarajan at the Information Sciences Institute. I am excited about problems in natural language processing, such as information extraction, named entity recognition, and how we can organize scientific literature to facilitate learning.

Prior to entering the Ph.D. program at USC, I studied Computer Science and Cognitive Science at UC Berkeley and worked at a start-up in San Francisco.
